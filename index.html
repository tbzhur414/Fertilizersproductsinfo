NP and NPK Fertilizers (Demand: ~0.34M tonnes/year)

Engro NP Product

Brand: Engro NP

Composition: 18% N, 18% P2O5

Production

Produced at Engro Fertilizers’ plants in Dharki, Ghotki, Sindh (100,000 tonnes/year), and Port Qasim, Karachi (100,000 tonnes/year). Total NP capacity: 200,000 tonnes/year.

Uses

Crops: Wheat, rice, cotton, sugarcane, vegetables, fruits.
Application: Basal at sowing for balanced N-P nutrition.

Importance

Ensures root and shoot growth; increases yields by 10–15% in cereals.

Urea Fertilizers (46% N, Demand: ~6.2M tonnes/year)

FFC Urea Products

Brands: Sona Urea Prilled, Sona Urea Granular, Urea Zinc Coated, Urea Neem Coated, Urea Tara

Production

Produced at Fauji Fertilizer Company’s plants in Goth Machhi, Sadiqabad, Punjab (635,000 tonnes/year), and Mirpur Mathelo, Sindh (718,000 tonnes/year). Total capacity: 1,353,000 tonnes/year.

Uses

Crops: Rice, wheat, maize, sugarcane, cotton, vegetables, orchards.
Application: Basal or top-dressing; zinc for rice/wheat, neem for sugarcane.

Importance

70% of Pakistan’s N consumption; improves yield by 10–20%, enhances sustainability.
