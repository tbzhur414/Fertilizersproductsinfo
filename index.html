Urea Fertilizers (46% N)

FFC Urea Products

Brands: Sona Urea Prilled, Sona Urea Granular, Urea Zinc Coated, Urea Neem Coated, Urea Tara

Production

Produced via Haber-Bosch process; zinc/neem coatings applied for efficiency. Zinc addresses soil deficiency; neem slows N release.

Uses

Crops: Rice, wheat, maize, sugarcane, cotton, vegetables, orchards.
Application: Basal or top-dressing; zinc for rice/wheat, neem for sugarcane.

Importance

70% of Pakistan’s N consumption; improves yield by 10–20%, enhances sustainability.

Engro Urea Products

Brands: Engro Urea, Zabardast Urea, NP (18:18), Zarkhaiz (NPK 8:23:18, 15:15:15, 17:17:17 SOP/MOP)

Production

Manufactured in Dharki/Port Qasim; Zabardast fortified with zinc; Zarkhaiz blended with SOP/MOP.

Uses

Crops: Wheat, rice, cotton, sugarcane, vegetables, fruits.
Application: Basal (NP/Zarkhaiz), top-dressing (Urea); SOP for tobacco, MOP for maize.

Importance

Zabardast corrects Zn deficiency; Zarkhaiz boosts NUE by 15–20%.

Fatima Urea Products

Brands: Urea Fatima, NP Sarsabz, NP Pakarab

Production

Urea from Sheikhupura plant; NP blended with phosphate sources.

Uses

Crops: Wheat, rice, maize, cotton, sugarcane.
Application: Basal (NP), top-dressing (Urea).

Importance

Balanced N-P supply; Sarsabz trusted for high-yield crops.

DAP Fertilizers (18% N, 46% P2O5)

FFC DAP Products

Brands: Sona DAP, Sona DAP Imported

Production

Reacting ammonia with phosphoric acid; imported DAP meets PS standards.

Uses

Crops: Wheat, rice, cotton, pulses, oilseeds.
Application: Basal at sowing; fertigation compatible.

Importance

Corrects P deficiency (80% of soils); boosts yield by 10–20%.

Uses

Importance

Zorawar improves P uptake in alkaline soils.
